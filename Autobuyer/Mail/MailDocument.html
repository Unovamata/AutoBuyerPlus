CloudFlare Turnstile Captchas & the Future of AutoBuyer+

TNT Implemented CloudFlare Turnstile Captchas on Main Shop Pages

Thank you for your continuous support in the development of AutoBuyer+.

The day has come, TNT has finally implemented Captchas on Main Shop pages. While AutoBuyer+ usage remains pretty much the same outside main shops, TNT is experimenting with the idea of utilizing captchas to stop botting across the board and this rises some alarms from our end when it comes to protecting our users.

To be specific, TNT has added CloudFlare Turnstile captchas. These can be automated but there a couple of caveats regarding the usage of the extension and its policies. AutoBuyers will still work in Neopets but these will require massive advancements to the way AutoBuyers handle requests and how captchas are taken in to account.

As end users we must consider the following points:

  • What other pages TNT will implement CloudFlare Turnstile captchas on?
  • Are CloudFlare Turnstile captchas running in other Neopets' pages that we are not aware of?
  • Will neopets be more aggresive when it comes to automation in the near future?

We're researching these changes and what areas of the game are affected by this update. We can assure you this will not be the last time TNT will implement something like this.

Thankfully, we are already looking at a solution that solves this problem outright.

 

 

So what now?

  1. AutoBuyer+ still works outside main shops: AutoBuyer+ is still working as expected. However, there will be several weeks of downtime with no updates.
    1. We don't want a "band-aid" fix. There may be other detectors running on the site, this can get our end-users banned and we don't want that.
  2. Deactivate AutoBuyer+ for About 1 Week or 2 and lay low: No big purchases, no automating tasks, no scripts, abstain from using the extension or any bot for that matter until the situation becomes clearer.
    1. TNT takes a while to act when they go about banning users. It's better to miss out on some NPs than losing your account outright.
  3. We will update the extension eventually: TNT has been doing massive changes lately and we are keeping up with these changes.
    1. We must do research and read through the code base to make informed decisions.
      1. What if we add an implementation that accidentally flags a system that automatically reports bot usage? That is what we're trying to avoid.
    2. The extension will return in the near future but all we ask from you is time so we can reinforce the extension while avoiding detection as best we can.

 

By keeping these points in mind, you can protect your account for the time being. So please, stay tuned to our Discord channels and if you have any questions, you can contact me directly @ZenAkiSen on Discord.
